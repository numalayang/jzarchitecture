ABOUT US

ARCHITECT-ENVIRONMENTAL PLANNER-ART DIRECTOR

A registered & licensed Architect, Environmental planner, Graphic designer and Artist. Graduate of BS Architecture and currently finishing his Master’s degree in Construction management.

Formerly worked in ABS-CBN, IBC 13 and GMA Network Inc. from 2001 to 2008 as an Artist/ Art Director/ Production Designer in some of the notable TV programs like Bubble Gang, Bitoy’s Funniest, Encantadia , Captain Barbell, Hokus Pokus, Nuts Entertainment, Kakaba-kaba and Pintados. He likewise had significant stints in selected theater shows, TV commercials, films both commercial & indi, and film festival parades, to say the least.

He was the Director and Deputy in-charge on Events operations of the United Architects of the Philippines- Qatar chapter for the fiscal year 2017-18. Currently, a Senior Architect in a private construction firm in Doha and the owner of JZ Santos Architectural Design and Construction in the Philippines.
